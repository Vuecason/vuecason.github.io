Vuecason is developing a rapid metal additive manufacturing method with minimal post processing. We use a novel induction FFF process to print pure metal through a nozzle - like plastic. This allows us to heat and place pure metal in one single step, eliminating the need for debinding or sintering. Our printer will be capable of producing large, high performance parts with really short lead times.
